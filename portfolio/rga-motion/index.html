Our Story

Where India's skies found their pilots

In 1998, a retired Air Force captain and three flight instructors opened a single hangar with two aircraft and one conviction — that world-class pilot training belonged in India, not overseas.

Today, Rajiv Gandhi Aviation Academy is one of the country's most trusted DGCA-approved flight schools. Our cadets fly with IndiGo, Air India, Vistara, and carriers across the Gulf and Southeast Asia. But our measure of success has never changed: the moment a student takes their first solo flight and lands as a pilot.

Application Process

Five steps from ground to sky

From application to your first flight in as little as six weeks.

  1. Step 01

    Submit Your Application

    Fill the online form with your academic records. Takes under ten minutes — no application fee.

  2. Step 02

    Entrance Assessment

    A short aptitude test covering physics, mathematics, English, and spatial reasoning.

  3. Step 03

    Counseling & Interview

    Meet our chief flight instructor. We assess motivation, discuss financing, and answer every question.

  4. Step 04

    DGCA Medical

    We guide you through your Class 2 medical with our partner examiners — usually cleared within three weeks.

  5. Step 05

    Enrollment & First Flight

    Secure your seat, receive your uniform and wings, and take your familiarization flight in week one.

Admissions

Eligibility

  • Minimum age 17 at enrollment (18 for CPL issue)
  • Class 12 with Physics & Mathematics (or NIOS equivalent)
  • DGCA Class 2 Medical (we assist with scheduling)
  • English proficiency for radio telephony
  • Valid passport or willingness to obtain one
