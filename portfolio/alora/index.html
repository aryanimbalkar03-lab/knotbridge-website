Our Journey

A Story Baked with Love

From a small kitchen in Mumbai to four stores across the city — every crumb has a tale.

Since 2018
2018

A Small Kitchen, A Big Dream

Chef Alora started baking from her tiny Mumbai apartment kitchen, selling to friends and neighbours. Word spread like butter on warm bread — her red velvet cake became legendary in the neighbourhood.

2020

Our First Store in Bandra

Despite the challenging times, ALORA opened its first store on Turner Road, Bandra. The cherry-red storefront became an instant landmark, drawing crowds for freshly baked sourdough and handcrafted pastries.

2022

4 Stores Across Mumbai

With overwhelming love from Mumbai, ALORA expanded to Andheri, Juhu, and Malad. Each store carries the same warmth and quality, with recipes perfected over thousands of batches.

2024

Award-Winning Artisan Bakery

Named "Mumbai's Best Artisan Bakery" by Food Critics Guild. Our commitment to zero preservatives, locally sourced ingredients, and handcrafted techniques sets us apart.
